About

I created a personal struggle, my own self doubt and pitty. I was down and doing all the wrongs things, things like always partying, eating like shit, avoiding family and friends, getting behind on bills, etc. One night at my lowest of lows I decided to close my eyes and asked myself why. I started to get a feeling that this wasn’t the me I remembered, my true self was just waiting to shine through. That night I made the decision to wake up the next morning and find that version of me again. This was my awakening, a realization that no ones is here to save me. I am the creator and I write my own story.

I spent years bettering my mental help which then lead to bettering my physical health, this is now apart of me and a daily practice. It’s never easy to realize you’re your own worst enemy, but if you can shed all the doubt and circumstances weighing you down you’ll begin to fly. This is my mission, to show you how to unlock your infinite potential.

My message is to inspire and elevate us all. Join me on this journey, use my words and experience, empower yourself with my brand ‘infinite religion’, and watch yourself transform into your truest self.

-Sage Ciryl
